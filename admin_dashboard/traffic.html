🚦 Traffic Prediction Module

Module A uses AI models such as LSTM and XGBoost to predict traffic patterns 30–60 minutes in advance. The system analyses historical traffic data, weather conditions, and live traffic inputs to forecast congestion.

Features

  • Predict future congestion zones
  • Traffic density heatmaps
  • Accident-prone spike detection

These predictions help traffic authorities take preventive measures before congestion occurs.
