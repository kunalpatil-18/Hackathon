🌿 Pollution Aware Routing

Traditional navigation apps only suggest the fastest route. Our system suggests the healthiest route by analyzing environmental conditions.

How the Route is Calculated

  • AQI (Air Quality Index) levels
  • Traffic density
  • Roadside pollution data

Who Benefits

  • Asthma patients
  • Cyclists
  • School buses
  • Senior citizens

By reducing exposure to polluted areas, the system improves public health while also optimizing traffic.
